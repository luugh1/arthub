Our Story


Art Collab was founded by Jon Snow, Dany Targaryan, Margery Tyrell and Robb Stark, four lifelong friends and artists who met in art school. With a shared passion for diverse artistic styles, they envisioned a platform where artists could connect, collaborate, and grow. Art Collab began in 2020, during the global pandemic, as an online gallery and quickly evolved into a vibrant community offering curated exhibitions, workshops, and resources. Today, it stands as a testament to their belief in the transformative power of art, providing a welcoming space for artists of all levels to explore, learn, and inspire each other.
